Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

1. Introduction

DeployDevOps ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our application and services (the "Service").

2. Information We Collect

When you authorize the Service to connect with your social media accounts, we may collect:

  • Profile Information: Your display name, username, profile picture, and account identifiers from connected platforms (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn).
  • Content Data: Information related to content we publish on your behalf, including post text, media files, and scheduling metadata.
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics about how you interact with the Service, including features used and timestamps.

We do not collect:

  • Passwords or authentication credentials (we use OAuth tokens provided by platforms).
  • Private messages or direct messages from any platform.
  • Financial or payment information.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Publish and schedule content on connected social media platforms.
  • Display your connected account information within the Service.
  • Improve and maintain the Service.
  • Respond to your requests or inquiries.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share data only:

  • With third-party platforms (TikTok, YouTube, etc.) as necessary to provide the Service (e.g., publishing content to your account).
  • When required by law, legal process, or government request.
  • To protect our rights, safety, or property.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on self-hosted infrastructure. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted storage of API tokens and credentials.
  • Access controls limiting who can view stored data.
  • Regular security reviews of our infrastructure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the Service. When you disconnect your account or request deletion:

  • OAuth tokens are revoked and deleted immediately.
  • Profile information is deleted within 30 days.
  • Content metadata may be retained in anonymized form for analytics.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Revoke Access: Disconnect your social media accounts at any time through the respective platform's settings or through the Service.

8. Third-Party Platforms

The Service integrates with third-party platforms governed by their own privacy policies:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • YouTube/Google Privacy Policy
  • Instagram Privacy Policy
  • Facebook Privacy Policy
  • LinkedIn Privacy Policy

We encourage you to review these policies as we are not responsible for their practices.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
